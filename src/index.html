Monty Hall Simulator

(1) In the Monty Hall problem, one prize is hidden behind one of several doors (three or more).

(2) You first choose one door. The door stays closed.

(3) Monty knows where the prize is, leaves your door and one other door closed, and opens every losing door he can. In other words, exactly one of the two remaining doors has the prize.

At that point, should you switch doors?
